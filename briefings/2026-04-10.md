## China / Taiwan

Adding to Taiwan's internal vulnerabilities, the opposition-controlled legislature continues to block President Lai's $40 billion supplementary defense budget, leaving procurement of critical systems — including HIMARS and anti-tank missiles — stalled at a fraction of the requested funding.
