## Domestic US Security

On May 21, 2026, CISA — alongside the NSA, Australia's ASD/ACSC, and other Five Eyes partners — published joint guidance titled "Careful Adoption of Agentic Artificial Intelligence (AI) Services," warning organizations that agentic AI systems introduce expanded attack surfaces, privilege creep, behavioral misalignment, and obscure audit trails when deployed across critical infrastructure. The guidance recommends organizations begin with low-risk AI use cases, avoid granting broad access to sensitive systems, and integrate agentic AI risks into enterprise security models. Separately, CISA released five Industrial Control Systems (ICS) advisories on May 21 (ICSA-26-141-01 through -05), covering vulnerabilities in products including Hitachi Energy GMS600 and ABB Terra AC Wallbox EV chargers — both widely deployed in energy and transportation infrastructure. CISA has indicated it will conduct targeted assessments of CI Fortify readiness, with priority given to defense critical infrastructure including dams, radar systems, weapon systems, and satellite communications. The flurry of May 2026 activity reflects a sustained posture shift at CISA toward offensive resilience planning following a period of constrained operations during a 75-day DHS internal disruption earlier in the year.
